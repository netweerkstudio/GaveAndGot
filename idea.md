GaveAndGot is an AI-powered cooking platform that helps users discover meals based on the ingredients they already have at home. Instead of searching for recipes manually, users simply enter available ingredients such as “eggs, rice, tomato, cheese,” and the platform instantly generates realistic recipes, cooking steps, meal variations, preparation time, and optional nutrition information.

The platform combines AI recipe generation, smart ingredient matching, and SEO-driven recipe pages to create a scalable cooking discovery experience.

Core features include:

Ingredient-to-recipe generation
AI-powered meal suggestions
Filters for quick meals, budget meals, high-protein meals, vegetarian meals, etc.
Dynamic recipe pages optimized for search engines
Personalized cooking recommendations
Grocery list generation
Save favorite recipes
Mobile-first modern UI with engaging animations

The homepage experience should feel interactive and modern:

Ingredients visually animate as users select them
A smooth transition leads into a fullscreen recipe experience
Generated meals appear in an immersive scrollable interface
Clean, appetizing visuals with warm modern colors

The target audience is mainly English-speaking users in countries like the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia who enjoy discovering new meals, cooking creatively, and reducing food waste.

The brand identity:
“Give ingredients → Get meals.”

The goal is to make cooking easier, smarter, and more creative using AI and automation.
